This Is Not About Cheating. It Is About Learning.

Some people misunderstand tools like the LUCY. They think that if a tool helps you draw more accurately, then it must somehow be cheating. But artists have always used tools. Grids, rulers, reference photos, viewfinders, mirrors, projectors, camera obscuras, camera lucidas, and other optical tools have all been used to help artists study, practice, and create. If you have ever used a camera lucida, you know that one still makes mistakes. You still have to observe. You still have to adjust. You still have to draw carefully. That experience is great for honing hand-eye coordination because it helps you connect what your eye sees with what your hand does. Please do not trash the tool just because you do not understand what it is. The LUCY does not make the art for you. It helps you see better so you can draw better.
